This is an App providing Supervision & Connectivity

for an AOU unit of

Plastics Unbound ( www.plasticsunbound.com).
The AOU is a slave unit connected to an Injection Moulding Machine (IMM) by means of a control cable,
and the injection moulding tool of the IMM by means of tempering hoses.


Applying the AOU provides the ability to

concurrently injection mould multiple different small to mid-sized plastic parts in one and the same tool.

It also provides the ability to injection mould large plastic parts with thinner walls,

thus significantly less plastic material, than conventionally.
