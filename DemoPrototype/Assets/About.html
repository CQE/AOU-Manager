AOU

This is an App providing Supervision & Connectivity for an AOU unit of Plastics Unbound ( www.plasticsunbound.com).

AOU is a slave unit connected to an Injection Moulding Machine (IMM) by means of a control cable, and the injection moulding tool of the IMM by means of tempering hoses. The AOU is externally supplied with Circulating water (cooling), Pressurised air (valves), and Electric power (heating and pumps).

Applying the AOU provides the ability to concurrently injection mould multiple different small to mid-sized plastic parts in one and the same tool. It also provides the ability to injection mould large plastic parts with thinner walls, thus significantly less plastic material, than conventionally.
